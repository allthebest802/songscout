Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

The short version

SongScout is designed to be simple and privacy-friendly. We do not require accounts and we do not sell personal data.

What information is collected

Most features run directly in your browser. Any preferences or inputs may be stored locally on your device using browser storage so the site remembers your selections.

No tracking or advertising

SongScout does not use advertising trackers. If analytics are added in future, they will be minimal and privacy-conscious.

Third party services

SongScout may link to external music platforms such as Spotify, YouTube or other music databases. These services operate under their own privacy policies.

Contact

If you have questions about privacy, please contact via the website contact page.
