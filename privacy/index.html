Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Simple and private

SongScout is designed to help people discover music without collecting personal information. No account is required and we do not sell personal data.

What information is stored

Some tools may store preferences locally in your browser so the site remembers your selections. This information stays on your device and is not sent to any server.

No advertising trackers

SongScout does not use advertising networks or invasive tracking technology.

External music platforms

SongScout may link to external platforms such as Spotify or YouTube. These websites operate under their own privacy policies.

Contact

If you have questions about privacy, please contact via the website homepage.
