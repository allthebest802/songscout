Editor's picks

Start here

Not sure where to begin? These six tracks are the perfect first step into classic rock and metal.

01
Stairway to Heaven
Led Zeppelin · 1971
Eight minutes from acoustic fingerpicking to full rock apocalypse. The guitar solo is consistently voted the greatest ever recorded.
02
Bohemian Rhapsody
Queen · 1975
Opera, ballad, hard rock — all in one song. No band before or since has attempted anything like it and pulled it off.
03
Smells Like Teen Spirit
Nirvana · 1991
The song that changed everything overnight. Kurt Cobain accidentally ended the 80s and started the 90s in four minutes.
04
Master of Puppets
Metallica · 1986
Eight and a half minutes of the most technically precise and emotionally intense music a band their age had ever made.
05
Purple Haze
Jimi Hendrix · 1967
Guitar playing that seemed impossible in 1967 and still sounds alien today. Every rock guitarist since owes Hendrix everything.
06
Comfortably Numb
Pink Floyd · 1979
Two guitar solos by David Gilmour widely considered among the greatest ever played. A meditation on disconnection that still breaks hearts.

Built by a music fan

SongScout was built out of a love for rock, metal and alternative music — and frustration at watching younger generations miss out on music that would genuinely change how they hear everything.

The reaction video community showed something remarkable: when younger listeners hear Led Zeppelin, Queen, Nirvana or Metallica for the first time, the response is always the same. Jaw-dropping. How did I not know this existed?

SongScout exists to make those discoveries easier — with context, curation and the connections that turn a single song into a whole new world of music.

Common questions

Music discovery FAQ

Quick answers to help you explore new music and understand how SongScout works.

How can I discover new music?
SongScout helps you discover new music by exploring genres, decades and artist influences. Start with the Era Explorer to find essential tracks, or use the Discovery Engine to see which artists inspired the music you already enjoy.
What is the best way to start listening to classic rock?
Start with influential songs from the 1970s and 1980s. Many modern bands were inspired by artists like Led Zeppelin, Queen and Pink Floyd. SongScout helps you find the key tracks that shaped the genre.
How do I find artists similar to my favourite band?
Use the Discovery Engine to explore connections between artists. Many bands are influenced by earlier musicians, creating a family tree of sound that makes discovering similar artists easier.
Is SongScout free?
Yes. SongScout is completely free to use. No login, no subscription and no ads.
What genres does SongScout cover?
SongScout focuses mainly on rock, metal and alternative music, helping listeners explore the most influential artists and essential tracks across multiple decades.
Is SongScout good for reaction videos?
Yes. Many tracks included are chosen because they create strong first-time reactions, making them ideal for YouTube reaction content and music exploration videos.
